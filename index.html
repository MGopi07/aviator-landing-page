THE GAME

What is Aviator?

Aviator is a multiplayer crash game where a virtual plane takes off while the multiplier continuously increases.

Players choose when to cash out before the plane flies away. The longer they wait, the higher the multiplier—but if the plane flies away first, the bet is lost.

"This simple yet exciting concept has made Aviator one of the highest-performing games in the iGaming industry."
